Applying to college can be difficult. It can be especially difficult trying to do so while being a first-generation and minority student. I made this website in the hope of helping those of us who don't have the resources. Being a first-gen student myself, I know the struggles and the fear of the college application process. I plan on using my experiences to build a website that is always accessible, easy-to-use, and is made for students like us. I hope this website helps you with the college application process.

Please Note: This website is a work in progress so feel free to give me any feedback you come across. Thank you and I look forward to helping you in your college journey as best as I can!

College Access Made Easy

College is basically a year-long process. It will take a lot of time, effort, and belief in yourself to get to college. But after going through 4 years of college myself, I believe that anyone who truly wants to achieve their dream of going to college can do it!

However, it is rare to find resources designed for first-generation and minority students. We are often supposed to understand and process information by spaces that were not built with us in mind. That is why I created this website. I want to provide first-gen students with the tools needed to achieve their dream, finish college, and get the job of their dreams.
